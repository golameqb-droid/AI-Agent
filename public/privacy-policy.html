Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6 June 2026

SocialAI Pro (“we”, “our”, “us”) provides AI-powered messaging automation for businesses on Facebook Messenger, WhatsApp, and Instagram. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our platform at socialaipro-app.equestionbankbd.com.

1. Information we collect

  • Account data: business name, owner name, email, phone, and login credentials you provide when registering.
  • Channel data: Facebook Page ID, access tokens, WhatsApp phone number ID, and Instagram account IDs you connect via Meta OAuth or manual setup.
  • Message data: customer messages, comments, and metadata received through Meta webhooks (sender ID, message text, timestamps).
  • Business content: knowledge base text, product catalog, and settings you upload in the dashboard.
  • Usage data: AI token usage, reply logs, and platform analytics for billing and service improvement.

2. How we use information

  • Generate and send AI-assisted replies on your connected channels.
  • Operate inbox, orders, comments, and analytics features in your dashboard.
  • Process subscriptions and payment confirmations.
  • Maintain security, prevent abuse, and improve the service.

3. Third-party services

We integrate with:

  • Meta (Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp): to receive and send messages on your behalf, subject to Meta’s Privacy Policy.
  • AI providers (e.g. Google Gemini, Groq, Anthropic): to process message content and generate replies. Message text may be sent to the AI provider configured by the platform or vendor.
  • Payment providers (bKash, Nagad, SSLCommerz): when you submit payment details for subscription.

4. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with service providers necessary to operate the platform, when required by law, or with your explicit consent.

5. Data retention

We retain account and message data while your account is active. You may request deletion of your vendor account and associated data by contacting us. Message logs may be retained for a limited period for support and billing purposes.

6. Security

We use HTTPS, access controls, and encrypted storage for sensitive credentials. Vendors are responsible for keeping their Meta tokens and passwords secure.

7. Your rights

You may access, correct, or delete your account information by logging into the dashboard or contacting us. End-customers who message your business page should contact your business directly for data requests related to those conversations.

8. Children

SocialAI Pro is a business service and is not directed at children under 13.

9. Changes

We may update this policy. The “Last updated” date at the top will reflect any changes.
