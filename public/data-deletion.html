User Data Deletion

Last updated: 6 June 2026

SocialAI Pro lets businesses connect Facebook Messenger, WhatsApp, and Instagram. If you want your data removed from our platform, follow the steps below.

For business owners (SocialAI Pro vendors)

  1. Send an email to golamrabbi620@gmail.com with the subject Delete my SocialAI Pro account.
  2. Include your registered business name and email address.
  3. We will delete your vendor account, connected channel settings, knowledge base, and message logs within 30 days.
  4. You should also disconnect the app from your Facebook Page in Meta Business settings.

For customers who messaged a business page

If you are a customer who chatted with a business using SocialAI Pro, your messages are stored on that business’s inbox. Please contact the business directly to request deletion of your conversation data.

Businesses can delete conversations from their SocialAI Pro dashboard or email us to request full vendor data removal.

Facebook / Meta data

To remove SocialAI Pro’s access to your Facebook data, go to Facebook Settings → Apps and Websites and remove the equestionbankbd app.
